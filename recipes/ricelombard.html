Rice Lombard

Rice Lombard is a very easy to make 15th century English dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups rice
  • 3 cups broth
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • pinch saffron
  • pinch cinnamon
  • pinch sugar

Preparation:

  1. Put broth, salt, and saffron into a large saucepan and bring to a boil.
  2. Add rice, cover, and reduce heat.
  3. Cook for about 15 minutes, or until rice is tender.
  4. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
